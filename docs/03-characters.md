# Characters

## Overview

There are two classes of character in the Meerklets visualisation:

1. **Meerklets** — Robotic AI agents based on AutoSergei's design. Small, numerous, task-specific.
2. **Meerkats** — The "real" meerkat characters from Compare the Market. Full fur, personality, character. They are the humans of the story — the engineers who build and run the factory.

The visual contrast between the two is critical: Meerklets are mechanical and uniform (differentiated by vest colour), while Meerkats are organic, individual, and full of personality.

## Meerklets (AI Agents)

### Visual Design (Based on AutoSergei)

**Reference**: See `reference/images/characters/autosergei.jpg`

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Head** | Real meerkat head with glasses. One lens has a **glowing blue LED** indicator showing the agent is active/thinking |
| **Body** | Sleek **white/silver robotic suit** — clean, slightly rounded futuristic form factor |
| **Chest stripe** | Vertical **pink-to-purple gradient LED stripe** running down the centre of the chest (signature AutoSergei element) |
| **Accents** | Small **cyan/teal glowing dots** on the body as status indicators |
| **Collar** | Black neckband/collar separating head from body |
| **Badge** | Small role identifier on the shoulder (B, T, D, R, A, M) |
| **High-vis vest** | Semi-transparent coloured vest over the white suit, indicating role. The vest allows the LED stripe and body details to show through |
| **Scale** | ~60-70% the size of the "real" meerkat characters |
| **Movement** | Purposeful, slightly mechanical. Efficient rather than emotional |

### Meerklet Color Logic

All Meerklets share a unified CTM identity with floor-specific accents:

**Base chassis**: All Meerklets have a **CTM Blue** (`#2843d0`) core body color — this marks them as part of the CTM fleet regardless of floor assignment.

**Floor-specific vests**:
| Floor | Vest Colour | Hex | Meaning | Roles |
|-------|------------|-----|---------|-------|
| **Floor 1** (Infrastructure) | **Bright Orange** | `#e8722a` | "Power" and stability | Deployers, Monitors |
| **Floor 2** (Production) | **CTM Yellow** | `#e6b30d` | "Value" being created | Builders, Testers |
| **Floor 3** (Orchestration) | **CTM Magenta** | `#d946a8` | "Brain" and connectivity | Architects, Reviewers |

> **Floor 4** has no Meerklets — only the real meerkat characters. The human floor is Meerklet-free by design.

### Meerklet Roles & Behaviours

| Role | Floor | Badge | Behaviour |
|------|-------|-------|-----------|
| **Builder** | 2 (Yellow) | B | Rapid typing, constructing code blocks, assembling components |
| **Tester** | 2 (Yellow) | T | Inspecting with magnifying glass, running diagnostics, checking outputs |
| **Deployer** | 1 (Orange) | D | Operating levers, pushing packages onto conveyor belts, managing pipelines |
| **Monitor** | 1 (Orange) | M | Watching dashboard screens, adjusting dials, tracking metrics |
| **Architect** | 3 (Magenta) | A | Drawing blueprints on holographic surfaces, designing workflows |
| **Reviewer** | 3 (Magenta) | R | Reading holographic documents, stamping approvals, flagging issues |

### Meerklet Lifecycle (Echoing Thronglets)
1. **Assembly**: Built on Floor 2's production line from components — blue chassis constructed first
2. **Training**: Absorb knowledge in training chambers (swirling data streams)
3. **Testing**: Pass through quality gates before deployment
4. **Deployment**: Assigned to a floor, given their floor-colored vest and role badge
5. **Operation**: Work their station, performing their role
6. **Multiplication**: As the factory grows, more Meerklets are produced — the Throng grows

### Meerklet States
| State | Visual Indicator |
|-------|-----------------|
| Active/Working | Blue LED lens glowing, chest stripe lit |
| Idle/Waiting | LED dimmed, subtle breathing animation |
| Error/Blocked | Red LED flash, chest stripe turns red |
| Learning | Swirling data particles around head |
| Communicating | Small holographic speech bubble between Meerklets |

## Character Interaction Rules

1. **Meerkats never wear high-vis vests** — they are not agents, they are engineers
2. **Meerklets never speak in full sentences** — they beep, chirp, and display holographic status
3. **Meerkats address Meerklets by role** — "Builder, redirect to the testing lane"
4. **Scale difference is always visible** — Meerklets are noticeably smaller
5. **Texture contrast** — Meerkats have fur/fabric, Meerklets have metal/polymer
6. **Emotional range** — Meerkats show full emotion, Meerklets show simple states (happy LED, sad LED, working LED)
